Our Classes

We offer a variety of classes that strengthen your body and mind. The classes that we are currently offering are

  • Yoga
  • Pilates
  • Zumba

Our instructors are certified and highly-trained professionals. You will be pushed to your limits, but in a safe and fun environment and you will discover that you are capable of more than you ever imagined!

Our classes are offered on a first-come-first-served basis. So, please do your best to arrive early!

Yoga

Yoga is a total mind-body workout that not only burns calories and tones your muscles, it also combines stretching poses with breathing techniques and meditation. You will come out of our yoga classes having your body and mind strengthened and stretched!

Length: 55 minutes

We have extra, clean mats that we can loan out, but these are available on a first-come-first-served basis.

Pilates

Pilates targets the core with slow, precise movements, and breath control and is low-impact. It also has a strong mind/body connection, so if you like yoga, but want a more intense core workout then Pilates is for you!

Length: 55 minutes

We have extra, clean mats that we can loan out, but these are available on a first-come-first-served basis.

Zumba

Zumba is a total-body cardio and aerobic workout set to high-energy, upbeat music. You don't need to be a great dancer to enjoy and get the benefits of our Zumba classes. Ditch the workout, join the party!

Length: 55 minutes
